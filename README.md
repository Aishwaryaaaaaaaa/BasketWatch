# 🛒 BasketWatch  
### A Hyperlocal Inflation Tracker

## 🌐 Preview

👉 https://basketwatch.netlify.app

---

## 🧠 The Idea

Official inflation (CPI) is based on aggregated data, but real-world prices often feel different.

BasketWatch explores:

> *Does inflation measured in official data match what people actually experience in real time?*

---

## 📊 What This Project Does

- Tracks prices of everyday items (e.g., groceries, essentials)
- Collects data from platforms like Blinkit and Zepto  
- Compares trends across cities (Pune, Mumbai, Bangalore)  
- Builds a dataset of real-world price movement over time  

---

## 🧪 Data Collection

Since automated scraping is restricted:

- Prices are collected **manually every week**  
- Stored in structured Google Sheets  
- Converted into datasets for analysis  

This reflects the challenge of building **real-world datasets from scratch**.

## 🎯 Why This Matters

Most inflation studies rely only on government data.

BasketWatch introduces:
- ground-level pricing  
- behavioural insights  
- real-time variation across cities
